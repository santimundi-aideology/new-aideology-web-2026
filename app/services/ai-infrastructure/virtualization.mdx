# AI Infrastructure Virtualization

Virtualization in AI infrastructure enables organizations to maximize the utilization of expensive GPU resources while providing the flexibility and isolation needed for diverse AI workloads. AIdeology specializes in implementing advanced virtualization solutions that allow multiple users and applications to efficiently share GPU resources without compromising performance or security.

## The Need for AI Virtualization

Modern AI infrastructure faces several challenges:

- **Resource Utilization**: Expensive GPUs often sit idle between training jobs
- **Multi-Tenancy**: Multiple teams need access to shared resources
- **Workload Isolation**: Different projects require secure, isolated environments
- **Dynamic Scaling**: Workloads have varying resource requirements over time

## Virtualization Strategies

### Multi-Instance GPU (MIG)
Partition NVIDIA A100 and H100 GPUs into smaller instances, enabling multiple users to share a single GPU with guaranteed resources and isolation.

### Time-Slicing
Implement time-based sharing of GPUs for development and testing workloads that don't require dedicated resources.

### Dynamic Resource Allocation
Deploy intelligent scheduling systems that automatically allocate resources based on workload requirements and priorities.

### Namespace Isolation
Create secure, isolated environments for different teams and projects while sharing underlying infrastructure.
