## What is Extended Reality (XR)?

-   **Virtual Reality (VR)**: Fully immerses users in a computer-generated environment, blocking out the physical world.
-   **Augmented Reality (AR)**: Overlays digital information or virtual objects onto the user's view of the real world.
-   **Mixed Reality (MR)**: Blends physical and digital worlds, allowing virtual objects to interact with the real environment.
