## What is a Digital Twin?

A Digital Twin is a living virtual representation of a physical counterpart. It is continuously updated with data from its physical twin and uses this data to:

-   **Visualize**: Understand the current state and performance.
-   **Simulate**: Test "what-if" scenarios and predict future behavior.
-   **Analyze**: Identify anomalies, diagnose issues, and uncover optimization opportunities.
-   **Optimize**: Improve performance, efficiency, and reliability.
